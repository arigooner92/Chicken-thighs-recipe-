Freekeh with Grilled Vegetables + Spiced Sweet Potato Wedges

Servings: 4
Prep & Cook Time: 45 minutes

  1. Cook 1 cup freekeh in 2½ cups water for 20-25 mins. Chop 1 zucchini, 2 carrots, 2 scallions, 1 onion, toss with 2 tbsp olive oil, ½ tsp salt. Roast at 200°C (390°F) for 25 mins. Mix with freekeh.
  2. Side: Slice 2 sweet potatoes into wedges. Toss with 1 tbsp olive oil, 1 tsp paprika, ½ tsp cumin. Roast at 200°C (390°F) for 35 mins.
