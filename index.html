Middle Eastern Herb & Garlic Chicken Thighs + Crispy Smashed Potatoes with Za’atar & Garlic Yogurt

Servings: 4
Prep & Cook Time: 45 minutes

  1. Marinate the Chicken: Mix 8 chicken thighs, 4 cloves garlic (minced), juice of 1 lemon, 2 tbsp chopped parsley, 2 tbsp chopped mint, 1 tbsp chopped thyme or oregano, 1 tsp ground sumac (optional), 2 tbsp olive oil, ½ tsp salt, ¼ tsp pepper. Let sit 15 minutes.
  2. Preheat oven to 200°C (390°F). Bake chicken for 30 minutes. Rest 5 mins before serving.
  3. Side: Boil 500g baby potatoes until tender. Smash on tray. Drizzle with 2 tbsp olive oil, 1 tbsp za’atar. Roast at 220°C (430°F) for 25 mins. Mix 1 cup Greek yogurt, 1 grated garlic clove, juice of ½ lemon, salt for dip.
