Skillet Chicken Shawarma + Crispy Chickpea & Fattoush Salad

Servings: 4
Prep & Cook Time: 40 minutes

  1. Mix 4 sliced chicken breasts, 2 tbsp olive oil, 2 tsp cumin, 2 tsp paprika, 2 garlic cloves minced, juice of 1 lemon, ½ tsp salt. Cook in hot skillet 8-10 minutes.
  2. Side: Roast 1 can chickpeas tossed with 1 tbsp olive oil, 1 tsp paprika, salt at 200°C (390°F) for 25 mins. Mix with 2 tomatoes, 1 cucumber, ¼ red onion, 1 bunch parsley. Add toasted pita chips. Toss with juice of 1 lemon, 2 tbsp olive oil.
