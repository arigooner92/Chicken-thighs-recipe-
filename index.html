Aubergine Kuku (Persian Eggplant Frittata) + Crispy Halloumi with Pomegranate Molasses

Servings: 4
Prep & Cook Time: 45 minutes

  1. Peel and cube 2 aubergines. Fry in 2 tbsp olive oil until golden. Sauté 1 chopped onion in 1 tbsp oil. Combine with aubergine. Beat 6 eggs, add 2 tbsp chopped parsley, 1 tsp turmeric, salt, pepper. Pour over aubergine mix. Cook covered 15 mins or bake at 180°C (350°F) for 25 mins.
  2. Side: Fry 200g halloumi slices until golden. Drizzle with 1 tbsp pomegranate molasses before serving.
