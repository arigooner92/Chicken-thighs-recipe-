Lebanese Chicken with 7-Spice + Fragrant Basmati Rice with Crispy Onions

Servings: 4
Prep & Cook Time: 45 minutes

  1. Mix 8 chicken thighs, 1 tbsp Lebanese 7-spice, 2 tbsp olive oil, juice of 1 lemon, ½ tsp salt. Scatter 2 sliced onions in roasting pan, place chicken on top, sprinkle 3 tbsp sliced almonds. Roast at 200°C (390°F) for 35-40 minutes.
  2. Side: Cook 1.5 cups basmati rice with 3 cups water, 1 cinnamon stick, 1 bay leaf. Fry 1 sliced onion in 2 tbsp olive oil until crispy. Top rice.
